Joker

The Joker is villain who operaates in Gotham City. He is a homicidal artist and an agent of chaos, he is the embodiment of everything Batman fights against. The Joker is portrayed as a criminal mastermind. Introduced as a psychopath with a warped, sadistic sense of humor whose real name and identity is completely unkown, is characterised by his chalk-white skin and green hair. The clown finds humour in suffering of others.
